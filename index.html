Available Positions

Material Preparation Assistant

Prepare course materials, organize study resources, and create educational content for online learning platforms. No prior experience required - training provided.

Online Portal Support

Manage university online portals, assist users with technical issues, and ensure smooth operation of digital learning environments. Basic computer skills needed.

Research Support Assistant

Support faculty and researchers with data collection, literature reviews, and academic research projects. Perfect for detail-oriented individuals.

Online Proctoring Assistant

Monitor and proctor online examinations to maintain academic integrity. Flexible hours available, ideal for night owls or early risers.

Setup Instructions

The software must be installed on a computer/desktop device. Mobile devices are not supported for the CRM installation.

1

System Requirements

Ensure you are using a Windows computer or desktop device.

2

Complete Application

Fill out and submit your Remote Assistant Application on the left.

3

Download CRM Software

Once your application is submitted, you will be able to download our company's CRM software.

4

Installation

Once downloaded, locate the .msi file in your downloads folder and double-click to begin installation.

5

Follow Prompts

Follow the on-screen installation wizard to complete the setup.

6

Launch Application

After installation, launch the CRM software from your desktop or start menu.

7

Login Credentials

Use the credentials that will be sent to your registered email address within 24 hours.

8

Training Materials

All training materials and job resources are compiled within the CRM software.
